News and Offers

The Horsley Community Shop is reviving the Horsley village email. Whoop whoop, we hear you say... because the village emails were very popular and people found them extremely useful. Your shop will send emails on your behalf with your requests to give away items, ask for recommendations and inform about upcoming village events. If you would like to send a message to the community, please email comms@horsleyshop.co.uk. The emails may also include news from the shop.
