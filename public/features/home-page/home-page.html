The Horsley Neighbourhood Plan was launched at the Horsley Village Fete on 15 September 2018. It defines development proposals for the village and the 16 surrounding hamlets for the next 20 years. A 6-week consultation period now begins.

A full copy of the draft plan can be viewed on this website (click link below). A number of hamlet meetings will take place during October when villagers will have the opportunity to give their views.

Learn about the proposals, join in the debate and have your say as to how the villages of Horsley parish will develop over the next two decades.

The Parish Council is keen to share the proposals and hear views about them from those who live and work in Horsley Parish (Gloucestershire). They encourage residents to subscribe to the newsletter (see form on the right), although every effort will be made to include people who are not on email.
