The village hall is held in trust on behalf of the community and is a registered charity ( Registered number: 301549).
It's managed and maintained on a non-profit basis by the Village Hall Committee, which is made up of local volunteers, with the Parish Council registered as Trustee.
The committee meets on a quarterly basis in the village hall, generally on the second Tuesday in January, April, July and October, from 7-8pm. The meetings tend to cover maintenance, management and financial issues, as well as subjects raised by hall users.
