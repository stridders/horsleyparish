The Parish of Horsley is in Stroud District in Gloucestershire. The parish is a dispersed settlement in the Cotswold AONB and includes 16 small hamlets over an area of almost 6 square miles. It consists of some 300 properties and has a population of around 750. Horsley Village has a church, a primary school, a pub, a village hall, a pavilion which houses the community shop and a playing field and play area.
